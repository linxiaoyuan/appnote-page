SuJian

Your smart clipboard notebook

Features

  • Smart recognition of shared content from WeChat, Weibo, Xiaohongshu, Douyin, Bilibili, Zhihu, Taobao, and JD
  • Automatically extracts titles and links, saves as a note with one tap
  • Organize notes into collections by topic
  • Original links preserved for easy navigation back to source

Privacy Policy

SuJian respects and protects your privacy:

  • All note data is stored locally on your device only
  • We do not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information
  • Clipboard content is only read while the app is in the foreground to recognize shared content, and is never transmitted to any server
  • The app does not contain any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
